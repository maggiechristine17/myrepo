Tips on How to use this Digital Garden:
- Table of Contents
## Tags
- #clippings are directly clipped from the web. (Located in Clippings folder)
- #project are things to do.  (Located in Projects folder)
- #topic are collections of pages on a topic. Bottom right side of the page, see "Pages mentioning this page" (Located in Pages folder)
- Pages may not have a tag. They are simply notes. (Located in Pages folder)
- Guides indicate a particular pathway for viewing certain notes (Located in Guides folder)
- #archived_project have been completed. (Projects > Archived Projects)
